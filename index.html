Southeast CPC was established in November 2009 by Brian Carroll and is based on the outskirts of Kilkenny City. We have been providing a full range of professional transport management and training services nationwide ever since. Over that period we have built and maintained strong relationships with our clients and pride ourselves on the quality of service we provide. We are always delighted to meet new potential clients and would be happy to discuss your business needs.
